# Explore

Research, exploration, and investigation workspace.

## What Goes Here

- Research into technologies, approaches, or tools
- Exploratory investigations and prototyping
- Strategic analysis and competitive research
- Architecture spike explorations
- Codebase deep-dives and audits

## Structure

```
explore/
├── <topic>/           # Active explorations at root
└── dungeon/           # Archived explorations
    ├── completed/
    ├── archived/
    └── someday/
```

## Guidelines

- One directory per exploration topic
- Include a README.md in each exploration
- Use `camp dungeon crawl` to triage finished explorations into dungeon
